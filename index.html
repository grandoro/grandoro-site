About Us

Focused. Private. Strategic.

Grandoro Holding Limited is a privately held company incorporated in Mauritius.
Our mission is to steward capital responsibly and cultivate long-term growth across
diversified assets. We operate with a low-profile, high-impact philosophy, building
partnerships grounded in trust and strategic alignment.

Why Mauritius?

Mauritius offers a stable legal framework, a favourable business environment, and
robust international connections — an ideal base for global holdings and investment
activities.
